# Purpose

This document describes the key DAX measures implemented in the Potchi Potchi Business Intelligence solution.

The measures were designed to transform transactional data into meaningful business KPIs supporting executive reporting, sales analysis, customer behaviour, inventory monitoring and financial performance.

Where appropriate, measures were reused across multiple dashboards to ensure calculation consistency throughout the semantic model.

---

# Design Principles

The DAX model follows several design principles:

- Reusable business measures
- Consistent naming conventions
- Explicit aggregation logic
- Context-aware calculations
- Separation between base measures and derived KPIs

Base measures were created whenever possible before being reused by percentage calculations and derived metrics.

---

# Revenue

## Purpose

Calculates total sales revenue generated by completed transactions.

## Business Formula

Revenue = Sum of Sales Revenue

## Used In

- Executive Overview
- Sales Analysis
- Finance

---

# Gross Profit

## Purpose

Calculates profit after deducting the Cost of Goods Sold (COGS).

## Business Formula

Gross Profit = Revenue − Cost of Goods Sold

## Used In

- Executive Overview
- Sales Analysis
- Finance

---

# Gross Margin %

## Purpose

Measures business profitability relative to total revenue.

## Business Formula

Gross Margin % = Gross Profit ÷ Revenue

## Used In

- Sales Analysis
- Finance

---

# Net Profit

## Purpose

Calculates operating profit after deducting operating expenses and platform fees.

## Business Formula

Net Profit =
Revenue
− COGS
− Platform Fees
− Operating Expenses

## Used In

- Finance Dashboard

---

# Net Profit Margin %

## Purpose

Measures overall profitability after operating costs.

## Business Formula

Net Profit Margin =
Net Profit ÷ Revenue

## Used In

- Finance Dashboard

# DAX Best Practices

The semantic model follows several DAX best practices:

- Measures are preferred over calculated columns whenever possible.
- Reusable base measures minimise duplicated logic.
- Percentage KPIs reference existing measures rather than repeating calculations.
- Measures remain filter-context aware to support slicers and cross-filtering.
- Naming conventions follow clear business terminology for readability and maintainability.

---

# Summary

The DAX layer transforms Potchi Potchi's transactional datasets into meaningful business metrics that support strategic decision-making across all analytical dashboards.

By separating reusable business measures from derived KPIs, the model remains scalable, maintainable and aligned with industry best practices for Power BI semantic modelling.
